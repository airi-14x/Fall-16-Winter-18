Cosmog

Topics Details
  1. Description
  1. Cosmog (Japanese: コスモッグ Cosmog)
    • Psychic-type Legendary Pokémon introduced in Generation VII.
    • Evolves into Cosmoem starting at level 43.
    • Evolves into either Solgaleo or Lunala starting at level 53 depending on the game it evolves in.
  2. Biology
    • Cosmog is a small Pokémon with a gaseous body similar to a nebula.
    • It has two wisp-like extensions that serve as arms and an ellipse running down its body.
    • Its front and back are black in color.
    • It has yellow eyes and two blue cheeks.
  1. Trivia
  1. Facts
    • Cosmog has the lowest base stat total of all Legendary Pokémon.
    • At 0.2 lbs (0.1 kg), Cosmog is tied with Gastly, Haunter, Flabébé, and Kartana as the lightest Pokémon.
    • Cosmog has the smallest movepool of all Legendary Pokémon.
  2. Origin
    • Cosmog may be based on a molecular cloud, a type of nebula where star formation occurs.
    • Cosmog may be a combination of cosmos and smog.
